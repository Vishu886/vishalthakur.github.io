PUT YOUR PHOTOS IN THIS FOLDER
==============================

Use these exact file names and they will show up on the site
automatically (just refresh the page):

  my-photo.jpg    -->  your face photo (About section)
  project1.jpg    -->  picture for The Forgotten Asylum
  project2.jpg    -->  picture for Zorb Fight

Tips:
- JPG or PNG both work. If you use PNG, also change ".jpg" to ".png"
  in index.html on the matching line.
- Landscape (wide) pictures look best for projects.
- Portrait (tall) pictures look best for your face photo.
- If a photo is missing, the site shows nice artwork instead,
  so nothing ever looks broken.
